    Rules for submissions

    How to submit

    Please upload your work through the form and be sure to fill out all mandatory fields. Response times vary and may range anywhere from 1 week to 1 month. Anything longer, and we will post an announcement on our socials. We have a strick policy against plagiarism, be careful!.

    Follow the guidelines below to ensure your submission can be properly processed:

    • If you are submitting more than once, please do so in separate form responses. For example, do not submit both an essay and a short story in the same form response. You may submit up to two times per issue. If your work is rejected, please do not submit again until the next issue.
    • Please only submit work that is relevant to the current theme and/or history in some way. If it is not, it will be rejected. We will not consider work that does not fit the magazine's mission.
    • We will not accept any pieces containing bigotry of any kind. Though the art of sharing ones stories is addicting, we do not condone any creations made to belittle/mock marginalized groups of people.

    We cannot wait to read your work!
